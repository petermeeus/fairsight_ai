The Problem

Contracts are dense and filled with legal jargon that conceals unfair or fraudulent terms. Without affordable legal counsel, individuals and small businesses risk financial and legal harm.

Our Solution

FairSight AI uses advanced NLP and machine learning to flag suspicious clauses, detect metadata tampering, and provide plain-language explanations—empowering non-experts to review contracts with confidence.

Key Benefits

  • Speed: Instant scanning and risk scoring.
  • Efficiency: Automated detection of suspicious clauses.
  • Compliance: Cross-checks with industry and regulatory standards.
  • Clarity: Plain-language explanations for non-experts.

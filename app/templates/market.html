Market & Feasibility

The integration of artificial intelligence (AI) into fraud detection systems has revolutionized financial services, payment security, and regulatory compliance. However, a critical gap remains in consumer-facing AI contract verification tools. FairSight AI aims to fill this niche by providing user-friendly, real-time contract fraud detection for individuals and small businesses.

Market Size & Growth

  • AI in fraud detection is projected to grow from $12.1B (2023) to $108.3B by 2033.
  • Contract management software market: $3B in 2024, 13% CAGR through 2030.
  • Untapped consumer-facing segment offers significant disruption potential.

Competitive Landscape

Existing solutions like Resistant AI and ComplyAdvantage cater to large enterprises. FairSight AI differentiates by focusing on everyday consumers and SMEs, providing plain-language risk scoring and intuitive dashboards.

Technical Feasibility

FairSight AI leverages large language models (LLMs), NLP, and metadata analysis to flag anomalies. Our system integrates real-time scanning, explainable AI (XAI) frameworks, and secure cloud deployment to ensure both performance and transparency.

Revenue Model

  • Freemium: Basic analysis free, premium tiers for advanced features.
  • Subscription Plans: Individual and SME levels.
  • B2B Partnerships: APIs for e-signature platforms (DocuSign, Adobe) to embed fraud detection.

By aligning with regulatory demands for fair and transparent contracts, FairSight AI positions itself as a leading consumer-focused solution in a rapidly expanding market.
